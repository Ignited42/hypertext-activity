Ending 2: Black Parade

It's here. Everyone's here! Faces you recognize, faces you've forgotten, faces you've never met before. They are smiling, laughing with glee as the music drowns out everything else.

Your sorrows, your guilt, for a moment, you could finally slip away from it all and dance to your heart's content. The parade marches on and you march with them.

You stomp at every beat and clap on every note. Every song you sing is shared by hundreds of others. And so you march until the sun sets in the distant skies.

The music dies down...
