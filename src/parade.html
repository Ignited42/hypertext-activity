Parade

The closer you get to the source of the music, the louder it becomes. You can hear the cymbals crashing, feet stomping to a rhythm. A symphony of springs plucked against an army of brass.

A winding note, a long sigh. An outward spiral, a glass sky.

You shake your head, dispelling the reverie and mesmerizing song. In the distance, you see a group of figures where the the music seems to emanate from. They're too far to make out any details, except for that they're wearing black.

It's tempting to follow them, to let yourself be entranced by the music. To dance along with them to locales unknown. The question is, will you?
