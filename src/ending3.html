Ending 3: Bright Lights

The next thing you know, you've burst into a most wondrous sight. Small crystals line the walls and ceilings of the open-air cavern. Lights glitter and bounce around as miniscule drops of water pool and form streams.

That's not even mentioning the many flora and fauna: strange, undiscovered and thriving in this underground paradise!

This thrilling discovery does come at a cost; you can't go back. Not even the crystals here are bright enough to illuminate the darkness you've came from.

So you carry on, with a spring in your step, as you venture forward on a new adventure unlike anything anyone's ever seen.
