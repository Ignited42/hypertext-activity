Ending 1: Lost

But that has never bothered you, has it? Even with the suffocating darkness or the unknowable stares, that has never killed your spirit.

So you continue to wander, no destination in mind. You'll find sights indescribable, foreign scents and tastes, even memories to cherish.

But you never stay. No, because the forest calls and your thoughts begin to wander again.

A lost child wanders back...
